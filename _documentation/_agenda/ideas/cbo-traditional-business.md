# CBO Applied to Traditional (Non-Software) Businesses

CBO was conceived in a software engineering context, but the AFIN srl. visit surfaced a broader application: traditional businesses with fragmented, manual systems are arguably the highest-leverage CBO targets. They have more to gain from structure, more to lose from chaos, and no existing framework telling them what to do.

## The angle

Most factories and traditional SMEs run on institutional knowledge, spreadsheets, and paper. CBO as an "integrator framework" can:
- Map their existing workflows (the audit layer)
- Identify which parts are candidates for simple tooling vs. structural change
- Provide a decision registry for the modernization itself

The AFIN engagement ([[projects/afin-srl-modernization]]) is a live test of this.

## Potential

If CBO works well in a non-software context, the framework itself becomes more general-purpose than initially framed. Could be a consulting model: CBO audit + roadmap as a deliverable.
