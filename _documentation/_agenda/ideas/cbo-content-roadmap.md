# CBO as a Content and Promotion Vehicle

The idea: frame CBO as something Guido is actively building and document the journey publicly. Use that roadmap to progressively promote creative work — starting with software programs, eventually moving toward videogames (core passion).

## The arc

1. **Phase 1 — Framework building**: document CBO decisions, publish the integrator framework concept, build credibility as someone who thinks deeply about systems and tooling
2. **Phase 2 — Programs**: release small tools or programs built on top of CBO workflows; use the framework credibility to make them land
3. **Phase 3 — Videogames**: the actual destination; the earlier phases build the audience, the craft, and the public presence before the harder creative bets

## Why this order

Videogames are hard to promote cold. A pre-existing reputation for building thoughtful systems creates the kind of audience that will actually care about a thoughtful game.

## Open questions

- What's the content format? Writing? Video? Both?
- Does CBO get its own public presence (site, newsletter) or does it live under a personal brand?
- When does Phase 2 start — before or after CBO is more fully documented?
